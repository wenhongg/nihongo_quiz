# Lesson Notes — 25 March 2025

## Grammar Points

### 1. Passive Form (受身形 / うけみけい) — Deep Practice
- **財布を盗まれたことがありますか** — "Have you ever had your wallet stolen?"
- **世界の生活費はアメリカによって上げられた** — "The cost of living was raised by America" (formal passive with によって)
- Pattern: ～たことがありますか with passive = "Have you ever experienced ~?"

### 2. ～ために (In order to / for the purpose of)
- **オイルをあまり使わないために** — "in order to not use much oil"

### 3. Causative-Passive Form (使役受身 / しえきうけみ)
- **こどものとき両親に何をさせられましたか？** — "What were you made to do by your parents as a child?"
- **机の掃除以外させられなかった** — "I wasn't made to do anything other than cleaning the desk"
- **猫に何をさせられていますか？** — "What are you being made to do by the cat?"
- This is the key grammar point of the lesson — combining させる + られる

### 4. ～以外 (Other than / except)
- **学校の宿題以外に** — "other than school homework"
- **机の掃除以外させられなかった** — "wasn't made to do anything besides desk cleaning"

### 5. なら (If / in that case)
- **スマホなら** — "if it's a smartphone (then...)"

### 6. 何か vs 何も (Something vs Nothing)
- **何か買わされたことがありますか** — "Have you ever been made to buy something?"
- **何も買わされたことがありません** — "I've never been made to buy anything"
- Key pattern: 何か + positive / 何も + negative
